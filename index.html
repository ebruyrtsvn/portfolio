About

Designed to ship.
Built to last.

I'm Ebru a Senior Digital Product Designer based in Istanbul. My background is in industrial design, but I've spent the last 4.5 years working exclusively on digital products across mobile apps, web platforms, and B2B tools.

At Allianz Turkey, I operated in three modes simultaneously: embedded team member shipping features across four dev squads, solo designer owning entire product domains end-to-end, and design lead running usability tests, deep benchmark analyses, and shaping product strategy alongside stakeholders.

I care about the full arc from benchmark study to final handoff. I'm as comfortable in a sprint planning session as I am in Figma.

Outside of work: Game Design with Blender, UX writing, YouTube.
